Description

The Hill Country Squarebill is built for one thing: deflecting off rock, laydowns, and dock pilings to trigger reaction strikes from shallow Texas bass. Its wide, hunting wobble and silent-rattle balsa-style body make it a go-to from Falcon to Fork.

The square lip lets you crash it into cover and pop it free without hanging up, and the factory-tuned line tie means it runs true straight out of the box — no need to bend an eyelet on the water.

  • Square lip deflects off cover to trigger reaction strikes
  • Wide hunting action that calls fish from a distance in stained water
  • Internal rattle chamber for extra fish-calling vibration
  • Factory-tuned to swim true — castable right out of the package
