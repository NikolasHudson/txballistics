Real Ammo. Real People.

We started TX Ballistics for one simple reason: shooters deserve a dealer who actually has what they sell, prices it fair, and knows the difference between the loads on the shelf.

Built in the Hill Country

TX Ballistics began at a kitchen table outside of Austin in 2019, when a handful of frustrated range buddies got tired of "in stock" ammo that never shipped, mystery counts, and prices that swung with the headlines.

So we built the shop we wanted to buy from. Every round we list is sitting on our shelf, counted by hand. You reserve it online and pick it up locally — no games, no backorders, no surprises at the counter.

We're still small, still family-run, and still shooting the same ammo we sell to you.

— The Hudson Family, Founders
