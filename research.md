Website Layout & Feature Reference — LuckyGunner.com
Primary Color
The site uses a deep forest/hunter green as its primary color throughout. It appears in the top header background, navigation bar, category sidebar headers, filter panel headers, "Add to Cart" buttons, and footer. This green conveys a tactical/outdoors brand identity and is used consistently to create visual cohesion across all functional UI elements.

Overall Page Structure
The page uses a classic three-column layout on category/listing pages, with a narrow left sidebar, a wide center content area, and a narrower right sidebar. This maximizes use of horizontal space and keeps navigation and filtering always visible.

Header (Top Bar — Two Tiers)
Tier 1 (Top utility bar): This is the very top strip of the page. On the left sits the logo. In the center, three trust-building bullet points are displayed inline: "In Stock," "Fast Shipping," and "$100 Guarantee" — each prefixed with a green checkmark. This immediately signals reliability to the visitor. On the right side are two account action links ("My Account" with a lock icon, and "My Cart" with a cart icon), plus a phone number and email link for customer support.
Tier 2 (Primary navigation bar): A full-width dark green navigation bar sits below the header. It contains a home icon on the far left, followed by text navigation links: Bulk Ammo, Specials, Blog, Labs, About Us, and FAQ. On the right side of this bar is a prominent search bar with a "Search" button. The search field is wide and easy to find.

Breadcrumb Navigation
Directly below the header sits a simple breadcrumb trail (e.g., Home › Handgun Ammo › 357 Magnum Ammo). This helps users understand where they are in the category hierarchy and provides quick backward navigation.

Left Sidebar — Category Navigation
A fixed left sidebar spans the full height of the content area. The top of the sidebar features a styled header block (primary green background, white bold text) showing the current top-level category (e.g., "Handgun Ammo"). Below it is a scrollable list of subcategory links (individual calibers), each with a right-facing chevron arrow indicating they are clickable. The active/current page item is highlighted and styled differently from the rest. Lower on the sidebar, the category sections are separated by additional green header blocks for "Rimfire Ammo," "Rifle Ammo," "Shotgun Ammo," etc., each with their own sub-lists. A "Free Subscription" email signup widget with a small icon and an email input + "Sign Up!" button is embedded in the left sidebar as well.

Main Content Area — Product Listing
Listing header row: At the top of the product grid, a count of results is shown ("Viewing 50 of 52 In-Stock"). Below that is a toolbar row with pagination controls (page numbers + previous/next), a "Show [X] per page" dropdown, and a "Sort By" dropdown with multiple sort options including Price, Manufacturer, Cost Per Round, Bullet Weight, Quantity, Brass Type, and more.
Product cards: Each product is displayed in a horizontal card layout (not a grid of tiles). Each card contains:

A product image on the far left (clickable, links to product detail page)
A "play video" link beneath some images for products that have demo videos
A "TESTED" badge stamp image for products that have been lab-tested
The product title as a prominent hyperlink (e.g., "357 Mag - 158 Grain JSP - Federal - 1000 Rounds")
A star rating with a review count link
The total price in large text
A smaller "cost per round" figure displayed separately in a muted pill/badge
A green "Add to Cart" button with a cart icon (this is the primary CTA — stands out strongly)
A stock count badge in the upper-right corner of the card showing quantity available (e.g., "13 IN STOCK"), styled with a folded-corner effect to make it look like a tag
A bullet-point list of key product specs below the price: quantity, manufacturer, bullet type, casing type

For sale/discounted items, both the regular price (struck through) and the special price (displayed in a red/orange accent color) are shown side by side.

Right Sidebar — Utility Widgets & Filters
Ship Next Day widget: Near the top of the right sidebar is a prominent "SHIP NEXT DAY" callout with a delivery truck graphic and a ZIP code entry field with a "See Estimate" button. This is a strong conversion feature.
Filter Results panel: Below the shipping widget is a comprehensive filtering panel titled "Filter Results by:" with a primary-colored header bar. Filters are organized in collapsible accordion sections, each with a bold category label, including: Quantity, Manufacturer, Brand, Use Type, Bullet Type, Bullet Weight, Ammo Casing, Ammo Caliber, Primer Type, Condition, Price (with price range links), and Attracts Magnet. Each filter option is a checkbox with the option name and a count of matching products in parentheses. The filter panel has "Close" and "Apply" buttons at the bottom.
Happy Customers widget: Lower in the right sidebar is a customer testimonials section titled "HAPPY CUSTOMERS" featuring rotating short quotes attributed to screen-name customers.

Pagination (Bottom)
At the bottom of the product listing, pagination controls are repeated — same format as the top: page numbers, items-per-page dropdown, and sort controls.

SEO / Info Section
Below the product listing and before the footer, there is a short "Quick Facts" bullet-point section about the current caliber/product category. This is clearly designed for SEO purposes and provides basic educational info for the shopper.

Footer
The footer is a clean, full-width dark green bar (matching the primary color) containing the logo on the left and a row of flat text links across the full width: Join the Team, Free Targets, Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy, Gun Laws. Below that is a simple copyright line with a Site Map link. A BBB (Better Business Bureau) Accredited Business badge is displayed in the bottom-right corner as a trust signal.
